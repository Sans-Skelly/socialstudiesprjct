Chapter 1:

A New Beginning

"Mom! Mom! Where did you put my hatchet axe? My friends are going to hunt and I want to go with them. It looks rusty and has a wooden handle with tethers on the end of it. I looked everywhere for the hatchet axe, but I can't find it," I said. "It is in your room onder your bed," said Mom. She had a disconcerning look on her face, with a bit of worry as she harvests the hide she got from the hunt her tribe got.

Gomda ran out the door with his trusty hatchet axe and joined his pack of friends to hunt some of the most delicious buffalo meat people rumored about
