After dinner, we ran out of the teepee and sat on the logs right near the bonfire where we sing songs that we love so very much. I shared the story about our hunt with the buffalo, and the tribe's members were hyped and intrigued as I progressed with how we took the buffalo down. We then finished our night chores and then I went to sleep.
